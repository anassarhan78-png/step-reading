Passage 10 · My Day (مع الصور + استماع ناطق)

⏰ Meet Khalid and his daily routine

🌅 paragraph 1
My name is Khalid. I am seven years old. I wake up at six o'clock in the morning. First, I wash my face and brush my teeth. Then I eat breakfast with my family. I usually have bread and cheese.
🎒 paragraph 2
After breakfast, I put on my uniform. I take my bag and go to school. I walk with my older brother. School starts at seven thirty. I like my school. My favorite subject is art.
🍽️ paragraph 3
At school, we have four lessons in the morning. Then we have lunch. I eat with my friends. After lunch, we play outside. I like to play on the swings. Then we have two more lessons.
📝 paragraph 4
School finishes at two o'clock. I go back home. I change my clothes and eat a snack. Then I do my homework. Sometimes my mother helps me. After homework, I play with my toys.
🍲 paragraph 5
In the evening, we have dinner together. My father comes home from work. We talk about our day. After dinner, I watch TV for a little while. Then I brush my teeth and go to bed at eight o'clock. I like my daily routine.

📘 STEP-style questions (A1) - Interactive

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 3, what does "lessons" mean?
2 Go back to paragraph 2: How does Khalid go to school?
3 Main idea: What is the main idea of this story?
4 Detail: What time does Khalid go to bed?
5 Inference: Why does Khalid's mother help him?
