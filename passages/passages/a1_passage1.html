🌼 paragraph 1
Sam goes to the park. The park is big and green. He sees tall trees and green grass. The sun is warm and yellow. Sam feels happy. He sits on a bench and looks at the ducks in the pond. There are five white ducks.

🧸 paragraph 2
Children play on the grass. A girl throws a blue ball. A boy runs after it. Two girls jump rope. They laugh and shout. Sam watches them. He remembers playing with his friends. The park is full of happy sounds.

🐕 paragraph 3
A woman walks her dog. The dog is small and brown. It runs fast and wags its tail. The dog sees a squirrel and barks. The squirrel climbs up a tree. The woman laughs. She gives the dog a treat. The dog is happy.

🧺 paragraph 4

🌇 paragraph 5
