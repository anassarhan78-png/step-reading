Passage 7 · The Impact of Social Media

📱 Connected or Distracted?

Social media has become an integral part of modern life. Platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and TikTok allow people to connect, share moments, and access information instantly. For many, these networks are a source of entertainment, news, and even income. But alongside the benefits, there are growing concerns about their negative effects.

On the positive side, social media helps people maintain relationships across long distances. It gives a voice to marginalized groups and enables rapid sharing of important information during emergencies. Businesses also use these platforms to reach customers and build brands. For young people, it can be a space for creativity and self‑expression.

However, the drawbacks are significant. Many users experience addiction, spending hours scrolling through feeds, which can lead to procrastination and lost sleep. The spread of misinformation is another serious issue: false news travels faster than ever. Furthermore, constant exposure to carefully edited images can cause anxiety and low self‑esteem, as people compare their lives to unrealistic online portraits.

Experts recommend using social media mindfully. Setting time limits, following accounts that inspire rather than upset, and remembering that online life is often a highlight reel can help maintain a healthy balance. In conclusion, social media is a powerful tool – its impact depends on how we use it.
