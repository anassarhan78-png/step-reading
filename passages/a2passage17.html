👨‍🚀 My Dream Job: Astronaut

🚀 paragraph 1
When I grow up, I want to be an astronaut. I have loved space since I was a little child. The stars, the planets, and the galaxies are very interesting to me. I want to see the Earth from above.
🌕 paragraph 2
To become an astronaut, I need to study hard. I must learn science and math very well. Astronauts also need to be healthy and strong. They exercise a lot to prepare for space travel.
🔭 paragraph 3
In my free time, I read books about space. I also watch videos about rockets and astronauts. My father bought me a small telescope. At night, I look at the moon and the stars. It is amazing.
🛸 paragraph 4
Last year, I visited a space museum with my family. I saw a real rocket and space suits. I even tried on a helmet. It was a dream come true. I took many photos and bought a small model of a spaceship.
🌍 paragraph 5
I know it is not easy to become an astronaut. But I will work hard and never give up. One day, I want to travel to space and see our beautiful planet. I hope my dream will come true.

📘 STEP‑style questions (A2) – اختبر فهمك

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does “exercise” mean?
2 Detail (paragraph 3): What did the writer's father give him?
3 Main idea: What is the passage mainly about?
4 Detail (paragraph 4): What did the writer try on at the space museum?
5 Inference: How does the writer feel about his dream?
