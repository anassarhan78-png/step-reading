🍛 My Favorite Food: Kabsa

🍚 paragraph 1
My favorite food is Kabsa. It is a traditional rice dish from Saudi Arabia. People often eat it for lunch or dinner, especially on special occasions like Eid or family gatherings. Kabsa is famous for its rich flavor and beautiful color.
🥘 paragraph 2
To make Kabsa, you need rice, meat (usually chicken or lamb), and a special mix of spices. The spices include cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and black lime. These give the dish its amazing taste. Sometimes people add raisins or nuts for extra flavor.
👪 paragraph 3
Every Friday, my mother cooks Kabsa for the family. We all sit together on the floor around a large plate. We eat with our right hand, as is the tradition. It is a special time when we talk and share stories. I always look forward to Fridays.
😋 paragraph 4
The taste of Kabsa is unforgettable. The rice is soft and mixed with tender meat. The spices make it warm and slightly spicy. I love the combination of flavors. It is not just food; it is a part of our culture and identity.
🤝 paragraph 5
When I have friends from other countries over, I ask my mother to cook Kabsa. They always love it and ask for the recipe. I am proud to share my favorite dish with them. If you ever visit Saudi Arabia, you must try Kabsa!
