Passage 13 · My Favorite Sport (مع الصور + استماع ناطق)

🏸 Meet Fahad and his favorite sport

🏸 paragraph 1
My name is Fahad. I am nine years old. My favorite sport is badminton. I play it every weekend.
🏸 paragraph 2
To play badminton, I need a racket and a shuttlecock. I have a blue racket. My brother gave it to me.
🏞️ paragraph 3
I play badminton with my older brother in the park near our house. We play for one hour. He is very good.
🎉 paragraph 4
I am getting better. Last week, I won a game against my brother. He was happy for me.
💧 paragraph 5
Playing badminton makes me strong and happy. I want to join a badminton club one day.

📘 STEP-style questions (A1) - Interactive

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what is a "shuttlecock"?
2 Go back to paragraph 2: What color is Fahad's racket?
3 Main idea: What is the story about?
4 Detail: Where does Fahad play badminton?
5 Inference: How does Fahad feel about badminton?
