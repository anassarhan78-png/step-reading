Passage 15 · At the Zoo (مع الصور + استماع ناطق)

🦁 Meet Ahmed and his family at the zoo

🦒 paragraph 1
Last weekend, Ahmed went to the zoo with his family. He went with his mother, father and little sister.
🦁 paragraph 2
First, they saw the lions. The lions were sleeping in the sun. They looked big and strong.
🐘 paragraph 3
Then they went to see the elephants. The elephants were eating grass. Ahmed's sister liked them very much.
🐒 paragraph 4
The monkeys were Ahmed's favorite. They were jumping and playing. One monkey made funny faces.
🍦 paragraph 5
After seeing all the animals, they bought ice cream. Ahmed had chocolate flavor. It was a great day.

📘 STEP-style questions (A1) - Interactive

3 Main idea: What is the story about?
4 Detail: Which animal was Ahmed's favorite?
5 Inference: How did Ahmed feel about the zoo trip?
