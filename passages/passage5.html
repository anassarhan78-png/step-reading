🌳 STEP Reading · A1

Passage 5 · A Day at the Park (مع الصور + استماع ناطق)

☀️ Lina's fun day outside

🌼 paragraph 1
Lina wakes up early on Saturday morning. The sun is shining. She looks out the window and sees a blue sky. "Mom, can we go to the park today?" she asks. Her mother smiles and says, "Yes, we can go after breakfast." Lina is very happy. She eats her cereal quickly.
🧸 paragraph 2
At the park, Lina runs to the playground. She loves the swings. She swings high in the air. She feels like a bird. Then she goes down the slide. It is fast and fun. Other children are playing too. Lina makes a new friend. Her name is Sara. They play together.
🐕 paragraph 3
Near the playground, a man is walking his dog. The dog is white and fluffy. It runs and jumps. Lina asks, "Can I pet your dog?" The man says yes. The dog licks Lina's hand. It wags its tail. Lina laughs. "He is so cute!" she says. She gives the dog a gentle pat.
🦆 paragraph 4
Lina and her mother walk to the pond. They see ducks swimming. The ducks are brown and white. Lina has some bread. She throws little pieces to the ducks. The ducks quack and eat quickly. A small duck comes near her feet. Lina giggles. "Look, Mom! It's so close!"
🌇 paragraph 5
After a long day, it is time to go home. Lina is tired but happy. She holds her mother's hand. "I had the best day ever," she says. "Can we come again tomorrow?" Her mother laughs. "Maybe next weekend," she says. Lina smiles. She cannot wait to come back to the park.
