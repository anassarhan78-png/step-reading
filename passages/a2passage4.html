🌸 My Favorite Season: Spring

🌼 paragraph 1
There are four seasons in a year: winter, spring, summer, and autumn. Each season is beautiful in its own way. But my favorite season is spring. I love it because the weather becomes warm and flowers start to bloom.
☀️ paragraph 2
In spring, the days get longer and the sun feels warm. The trees grow new leaves, and the grass becomes green again. Many birds come back from warm places. I enjoy listening to their songs in the morning.
🌳 paragraph 3
During spring, my family and I often go to the park. We have picnics under the trees. My mother prepares sandwiches and fresh juice. My father plays football with my brother. I like to read a book or just relax on the grass.
🌸 paragraph 4
Another reason I love spring is the flowers. Red roses, yellow tulips, and white lilies fill the gardens. The air smells fresh and sweet. Sometimes I pick a few flowers and put them in a vase at home. They make the house look cheerful.
🌧️ paragraph 5
Spring also brings light rain. After the rain, the sky sometimes shows a rainbow. I love to see the colors in the sky. Spring makes me feel happy and full of energy. It is the season of new life and hope. That is why it is my favorite.

📘 STEP‑style questions (A2) – اختبر فهمك

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 1, what does “bloom” mean?
2 Detail (paragraph 2): What happens to the trees in spring?
3 Main idea: What is the passage mainly about?
4 Detail (paragraph 4): What does the writer sometimes do with flowers?
5 Inference: How does the writer feel about spring?
