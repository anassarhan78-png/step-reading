🐬 Dolphins: Wonderful Sea Creatures

🌊 paragraph 1
Dolphins are beautiful animals that live in the ocean. They are mammals, not fish. This means they breathe air like humans do. Dolphins come to the surface to breathe through a hole on top of their heads.
🐬 paragraph 2
Dolphins are very smart. They can learn tricks and understand commands. They live in groups called pods. Pods can have up to twelve dolphins. They work together to find food and protect each other.
🤸 paragraph 3
Dolphins love to play. They often jump out of the water and do spins in the air. Sometimes they ride the waves near boats. Watching dolphins play makes people happy. Many people go on boat trips just to see them.
💙 paragraph 4
Dolphins communicate with each other using sounds. They make clicks and whistles. Each dolphin has its own special whistle, like a name. Scientists are still studying how dolphins talk to one another.
🌍 paragraph 5
Unfortunately, dolphins face many dangers. Pollution in the ocean hurts them. Sometimes they get caught in fishing nets. We must protect these amazing animals. Everyone can help by keeping the oceans clean.
