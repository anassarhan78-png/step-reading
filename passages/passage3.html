Passage 3 · At the Supermarket

🏪 Let's go shopping with Samira

🛒 paragraph 1
Samira goes to the supermarket with her mother. The supermarket is big and bright. There are many aisles with different things. Samira takes a shopping cart. She pushes it slowly. She sees many people shopping. It is busy but fun.
🔊 listen
🍎 paragraph 2
First, they go to the fruit and vegetable section. There are red apples, yellow bananas, and green grapes. Samira picks some oranges. Her mother chooses fresh tomatoes and lettuce. The vegetables look very fresh. Samira loves the bright colours.
🔊 listen
🥛 paragraph 3
Next, they go to the dairy section. Samira sees many types of milk, cheese, and yogurt. Her mother puts a bottle of milk in the cart. She also takes some butter and cream. Samira likes strawberry yogurt. She puts one in the cart.
🔊 listen
🍞 paragraph 4
After that, they go to the bakery section. The smell of fresh bread is nice. Her mother picks a loaf of brown bread. Samira sees delicious cakes and cookies. She asks her mother, "Can we buy some cookies?" Her mother smiles and says yes.
🔊 listen
💰 paragraph 5
Finally, they go to the checkout counter. The cashier scans all the items. Samira helps put the things in bags. Her mother pays with a card. They walk home happily. Samira says, "I like shopping at the supermarket. It is always an adventure!"
🔊 listen
