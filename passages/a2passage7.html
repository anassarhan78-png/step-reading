👫 My Best Friend, Ahmed

🤝 paragraph 1
My best friend's name is Ahmed. He is twelve years old, the same age as me. We are in the same class at school. Ahmed has black hair and brown eyes. He is very kind and always helps me when I need him.
🏫 paragraph 2
We met on the first day of school three years ago. I was nervous because I didn't know anyone. Ahmed sat next to me and started talking. He asked about my favorite hobbies. Soon, we became good friends.
⚽ paragraph 3
Ahmed and I like to do many things together. After school, we often play football in the park. On weekends, we ride our bikes or play video games. Sometimes we do homework together at my house.
💪 paragraph 4
Last month, I was sick and missed school for a week. Ahmed visited me and brought my homework. He explained the lessons I missed. Thanks to him, I didn't fall behind in class. I was very grateful.
🌟 paragraph 5
A true friend is hard to find. I am lucky to have Ahmed in my life. We share secrets, laugh a lot, and support each other. I hope we stay friends forever. Everyone needs a friend like Ahmed.

📘 STEP‑style questions (A2) – اختبر فهمك

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does “nervous” mean?
2 Detail (paragraph 1): How old is Ahmed?
3 Main idea: What is the passage mainly about?
4 Detail (paragraph 4): Why did Ahmed visit the writer last month?
5 Inference: How does the writer feel about Ahmed?
