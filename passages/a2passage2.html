📖 The Joy of Reading

📚 paragraph 1
Reading is one of the most enjoyable hobbies. Many people love to read in their free time. It helps you relax and learn new things. You can read anywhere: at home, in a park, or on a bus.
📖 paragraph 2
There are many types of books. Some people enjoy fiction stories, like mystery or romance. Others prefer non‑fiction, such as history or science books. Every reader can find something they like.
☕ paragraph 3
I usually read before I go to sleep. It helps me forget about the day and enter a different world. My favorite place to read is a quiet coffee shop near my house. I order a warm drink and read for an hour.
🏛️ paragraph 4
Last week, I visited the public library. I borrowed two novels and a book about space. The library is very quiet, and there are many comfortable chairs. I spent the whole afternoon there.
🌳 paragraph 5
Reading improves your vocabulary and your imagination. It also makes you a better writer. I believe that everyone should read at least one book every month. It is a wonderful habit that stays with you forever.

📘 STEP‑style questions (A2) – اختبر فهمك

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does “fiction” mean?
2 Detail (paragraph 3): When does the writer usually read?
3 Main idea: What is the passage mainly about?
4 Detail (paragraph 4): What did the writer borrow from the library?
5 Inference: Based on the last paragraph, what does the writer think about reading?
