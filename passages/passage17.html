Passage 17 · My Classroom (مع الصور + استماع ناطق)

🏫 Meet Fatima and her classroom

🚪 paragraph 1
Hello! I am Fatima. I am in Grade 2. This is my classroom. It is big and clean.
🪑 paragraph 2
There are twenty desks and twenty chairs in my class. I sit next to my friend Amal. Our teacher's desk is at the front.
📖 paragraph 3
On the wall, there is a big whiteboard. Our teacher writes on it. There is also a bookshelf with many books.
🙋 paragraph 4
In my classroom, we read, write and draw. I like drawing the most. When the teacher asks a question, we raise our hands.
🧹 paragraph 5
At the end of the day, we clean our classroom. I water the plants. I love my classroom because I learn new things every day.

📘 STEP-style questions (A1) - Interactive

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does "desk" mean?
2 Go back to paragraph 2: Who sits next to Fatima?
3 Main idea: What is the story mainly about?
4 Detail: What does Fatima like to do the most in class?
5 Inference: Why does Fatima love her classroom?
