Passage 7 · My Daily Routine

🌅 A day in the life of Karim

🌄 paragraph 1
My name is Karim. I am eight years old. I wake up at 6:30 every morning. The sun is not fully up yet. I stretch my arms and yawn. Then I make my bed. I like to start my day with a clean room.
🔊 listen
🪥 paragraph 2
After making my bed, I go to the bathroom. I wash my face and brush my teeth. I use toothpaste with a mint flavor. It makes my mouth feel fresh. Then I comb my hair. I am ready for breakfast.
🔊 listen
🍳 paragraph 3
Breakfast is my favorite meal. I eat bread with cheese and olives. I drink a glass of milk. Sometimes I have eggs. My mother makes sure I eat well. She says breakfast gives me energy for school.
🔊 listen
🎒 paragraph 4
At 7:30, I put on my school uniform. I pack my bag with books and pencils. My friend Ali waits for me outside. We walk to school together. It takes ten minutes. We talk and laugh on the way.
🔊 listen
🍽️ paragraph 5
School ends at 2:30. I come home and have lunch. Then I do my homework. In the evening, I play with my brother. We have dinner together at 7:00. Before bed, I read a story. I sleep at 9:00. That is my daily routine.
🔊 listen
