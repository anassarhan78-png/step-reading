Passage 6 · My Pet Cat (مع الصور + استماع ناطق)

😺 Meet Nora and her cat Mimo

🐈 paragraph 1
Nora has a pet cat. His name is Mimo. Mimo is two years old. He has orange fur and big green eyes. Nora loves Mimo very much. Mimo is not just a pet. He is part of the family.
🍲 paragraph 2
Every morning, Nora gives Mimo food. Mimo likes fish and chicken. He also drinks fresh water. After eating, Mimo cleans his face with his paw. Nora watches him and smiles. He is very clean.
🧶 paragraph 3
Mimo loves to play. His favorite toy is a small red ball. He runs after it and jumps high. Sometimes he hides under the bed. Nora calls him, "Mimo, where are you?" He comes out slowly and meows.
😴 paragraph 4
At night, Mimo sleeps on Nora's bed. He curls up into a small ball. He purrs softly. Nora pets him gently. The sound of his purring makes Nora feel calm. She falls asleep quickly.
❤️ paragraph 5
Nora takes many photos of Mimo. She shares them with her friends. Everyone says Mimo is cute. Nora is happy to have such a good friend. She says, "Mimo is the best cat in the world!" She loves him every day.

📘 STEP-style questions (A1) - Interactive

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 4, what does the word "purrs" mean?
2 Go back to paragraph 2: What does Mimo do after eating?
3 Main idea: What is the main idea of this story?
4 Detail: What color is Mimo's favorite toy?
5 Inference: How does Nora feel about Mimo?
