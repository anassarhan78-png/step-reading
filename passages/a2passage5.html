📘 STEP Reading · A2

Passage 5 · A Visit to the Farm (نصوص متكاملة + استماع)

🐄 A Day at the Farm

🚜 paragraph 1
Last weekend, my family and I visited a farm. It was outside the city, in a quiet village. The farm was very big. There were green fields, tall trees, and many animals. I was excited because I had never been to a farm before.
🐑 paragraph 2
The farmer welcomed us and showed us around. First, we saw the sheep. They were white and fluffy. Some sheep had small babies, called lambs. They were so cute! The farmer let us feed them with grass.
🐄 paragraph 3
Then we visited the cows. The farmer explained how they milk the cows every morning. We even tried fresh milk. It was warm and creamy. I liked it a lot. After that, we saw the chickens. They were walking around and making sounds.
🥕 paragraph 4
The farm also had a big vegetable garden. There were carrots, tomatoes, and lettuce. Everything was fresh and organic. We picked some vegetables and took them home. My mother cooked a delicious salad with them for dinner.
🚜 paragraph 5
Before we left, the farmer gave us a ride on his tractor. It was fun to sit on the big machine. I learned a lot about farm life. It was a wonderful day. I hope to visit the farm again soon.

📘 STEP‑style questions (A2) – اختبر فهمك

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does “lambs” mean?
2 Detail (paragraph 3): What did the writer try at the farm?
3 Main idea: What is the story mainly about?
4 Detail (paragraph 4): What did the family take home from the farm?
5 Inference: How did the writer feel about the farm visit?
