👥 STEP Reading · A1

Passage 8 · My Best Friend (مع الصور + استماع ناطق)

🤝 Meet Youssef and his best friend Adam

👬 paragraph 1
My name is Youssef. I am nine years old. I have a best friend. His name is Adam. He is also nine. We are in the same class at school. We sit next to each other. Adam is my best friend because he is kind and funny.
⚽ paragraph 2
After school, we often play together. We like to play football in the park. Adam is very good at football. He runs fast and scores many goals. I am not as good as him, but he never makes fun of me. He always passes the ball to me.
📚 paragraph 3
Adam helps me with my homework. Sometimes I do not understand math. He explains it to me slowly. He never gets angry. I help him with spelling. We study together at my house. My mother gives us cookies and juice.
🍪 paragraph 4
Adam always shares his snacks with me. He gives me half of his sandwich. I share my chocolate with him. We laugh and tell jokes. When I am sad, Adam makes me feel better. He is a true friend. I am lucky to have him.
❤️ paragraph 5
This weekend, Adam is coming to my birthday party. I am so excited. We will play games and eat cake. I made him a special card. It says, "Thank you for being my best friend." Good friends are hard to find. I will always be Adam's friend.

📘 STEP-style questions (A1) - Interactive

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does the phrase "makes fun of" mean?
2 Go back to paragraph 3: What subject does Adam help Youssef with?
3 Main idea: What is the main idea of this story?
4 Detail: What does Youssef give Adam at the birthday party?
5 Inference: Why does Youssef think Adam is a good friend?
