Passage 2 · My Family

🏡 Meet Ahmed and his family

🏠 paragraph 1
My name is Ahmed. I am ten years old. I live with my family in a small house. The house has a kitchen, a living room, and three bedrooms. I love my home because my family is there. We laugh and talk together every day.
🔊 listen
👩‍🍳 paragraph 2
My mother's name is Fatima. She is a teacher. She works at a school near our house. She makes delicious food for us. My favourite dish is her chicken biryani. After work, she helps me with my homework. She is very kind and patient.
🔊 listen
👨‍💼 paragraph 3
My father's name is Khalid. He is an engineer. He builds bridges and roads. He is very strong and smart. On Fridays, he takes us to the park. We play football together. He also tells me stories before I sleep. I want to be like him when I grow up.
🔊 listen
👧👧 paragraph 4
I have two sisters. Their names are Sara and Noura. Sara is eight years old. She likes to draw and colour. Noura is only four years old. She loves to play with her dolls. Sometimes we fight, but we always say sorry. I love playing with them.
🔊 listen
👴👵 paragraph 5
My grandparents live near us. We visit them every weekend. My grandfather tells funny jokes. My grandmother gives us sweets and biscuits. She hugs us and says, "You are my favourite grandchildren!" I feel very happy when I see them.
🔊 listen

📘 STEP-style questions (A1)

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does the word "patient" mean?
(a) sick (b) calm and not in a hurry (c) fast (d) strong
2 Go back to paragraph 4: Read paragraph 4 again. How old is Sara? What does she like to do?
3 Main idea: What is the main idea of this story?
(a) Ahmed's school (b) Ahmed's family (c) Ahmed's city (d) Ahmed's friends
4 Detail: What does Ahmed's father do on Fridays? (paragraph 3)
5 Inference: Do you think Ahmed loves his grandmother? How do you know?
