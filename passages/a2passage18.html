🎬 My Favorite Movie: The Lion King

🦁 paragraph 1
My favorite movie is The Lion King. It is an animated film from Disney. I first watched it when I was five years old. I loved the characters and the music. I have watched it many times since then.
👑 paragraph 2
The story is about a young lion named Simba. He lives in Africa with his family. His father, Mufasa, is the king of the animals. Simba will be king one day. But his uncle Scar is jealous and wants to be king.
🐗 paragraph 3
After a terrible event, Simba leaves his home. He meets two funny friends, Timon and Pumbaa. They teach him to live happily and not worry. The three of them have many adventures together. Their song "Hakuna Matata" is very famous.
🌟 paragraph 4
When Simba grows up, his childhood friend Nala finds him. She tells him that Scar is a bad king. The land is suffering. Simba must decide: stay with his friends or go back and take his place as king. It is a difficult choice.
🌅 paragraph 5
In the end, Simba returns and fights Scar. He becomes the new king. The movie teaches us about courage, responsibility, and family. I love this movie because it makes me feel happy and sad at the same time. I recommend it to everyone.

📘 STEP‑style questions (A2) – اختبر فهمك

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does “jealous” mean?
2 Detail (paragraph 3): What is the name of Simba's funny friends?
3 Main idea: What is the passage mainly about?
4 Detail (paragraph 4): Who finds Simba when he grows up?
5 Inference: What lesson does the movie teach according to the last paragraph?
