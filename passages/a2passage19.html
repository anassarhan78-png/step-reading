Passage 19 · Using the Internet (نصوص متكاملة + استماع)

💻 Using the Internet Wisely

🖥️ paragraph 1
The internet is a big part of our lives. Many people use it every day for work, school, or fun. You can find information, watch videos, and talk to friends online. It is very useful, but we must use it carefully.
🔍 paragraph 2
I use the internet for my homework. When I need information for a project, I search for it online. There are many educational websites. I also watch videos that explain difficult topics. It helps me understand my lessons better.
📱 paragraph 3
The internet also helps me stay in touch with friends and family. My cousin lives in another city. We video call each other every week. It feels like we are together. Social media is fun, but I don't spend too much time on it.
⏰ paragraph 4
However, too much internet is not good. Sometimes I forget the time when I play games online. My mother reminds me to take breaks. She says it is important to play outside and read books too. The internet should not replace real life.
🏡 paragraph 5
In conclusion, the internet is a great tool. It helps us learn, communicate, and have fun. But we must use it wisely. We should balance our time online with other activities. That way, we can enjoy the best of both worlds.

📘 STEP‑style questions (A2) – اختبر فهمك

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 1, what does “useful” mean?
2 Detail (paragraph 2): What does the writer use the internet for?
3 Main idea: What is the passage mainly about?
4 Detail (paragraph 4): What does the writer's mother remind him to do?
5 Inference: What is the writer's advice about using the internet?
