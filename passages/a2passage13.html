Passage 13 · Going to the Doctor (نصوص متكاملة + استماع)

🏥 A Visit to the Doctor

🤒 paragraph 1
Last week, I woke up with a sore throat and a headache. I felt very tired and my body was hot. My mother took my temperature. It was 38.5°C. I had a fever.
👩‍⚕️ paragraph 2
My mother decided to take me to the doctor. We went to the clinic near our house. There were a few people in the waiting room. We sat down and waited for our turn. I looked at some magazines.
🩺 paragraph 3
Finally, the nurse called my name. We went into the doctor's room. The doctor was very friendly. He asked me how I felt. Then he listened to my chest with a stethoscope. He also looked at my throat and said it was red.
💊 paragraph 4
The doctor said I had a flu. He gave me a prescription for some medicine. He told me to rest at home and drink plenty of water. My mother bought the medicine from the pharmacy on the way home.
😊 paragraph 5
I stayed home for two days. I took the medicine and drank warm soup. My mother took care of me. After two days, I felt much better. I was happy to go back to school and see my friends again.

📘 STEP‑style questions (A2) – اختبر فهمك

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 1, what does “fever” mean?
2 Detail (paragraph 3): What did the doctor use to listen to the writer's chest?
3 Main idea: What is the story mainly about?
4 Detail (paragraph 4): What did the doctor tell the writer to do?
5 Inference: How did the writer feel at the end of the story?
