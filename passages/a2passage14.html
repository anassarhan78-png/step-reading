Passage 14 · My Favorite Sport (نصوص متكاملة + استماع)

⚽ My Favorite Sport: Football

🏟️ paragraph 1
My favorite sport is football. It is the most popular sport in the world. People play it in almost every country. You only need a ball and a place to play. Football is fun and exciting.
👦 paragraph 2
I started playing football when I was six years old. My father taught me how to kick the ball. At first, it was difficult. But I practiced every day. Now I can control the ball well and score goals.
👥 paragraph 3
I play football with my friends every weekend. We go to the park near our houses. We divide into two teams and play a match. It is a great way to spend time together and stay healthy.
📺 paragraph 4
I also love watching football on TV. My favorite team is Al-Hilal. I watch their matches with my father. Last year, we went to the stadium to see them play. It was an amazing experience.
🏆 paragraph 5
Football teaches me important lessons. It shows me how to work in a team and never give up. When I play, I forget about my problems. I hope to play football for many years. Maybe one day I will become a professional player.

📘 STEP‑style questions (A2) – اختبر فهمك

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 1, what does “popular” mean?
2 Detail (paragraph 2): How old was the writer when he started playing football?
3 Main idea: What is the passage mainly about?
4 Detail (paragraph 4): What is the writer's favorite team?
5 Inference: According to paragraph 5, what does football teach the writer?
