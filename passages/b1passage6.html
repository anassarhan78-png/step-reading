Passage 6 · The Joy of Reading

📚 Open a Book, Open Your Mind

Reading books is one of the most rewarding habits a person can develop. Whether it is fiction, history, or science, reading regularly offers a wide range of benefits that enrich both the mind and the soul. In a world full of screens and notifications, picking up a book can be a powerful way to slow down and focus.

First, reading improves knowledge and vocabulary. Every book exposes the reader to new ideas, facts, and ways of expressing thoughts. This mental stimulation keeps the brain active and can even delay cognitive decline in old age. Moreover, reading fiction helps develop empathy: when we follow characters’ lives, we learn to understand different perspectives and emotions.

On a personal level, reading is an excellent tool for relaxation. Getting lost in a good story reduces stress and transports the mind away from daily worries. Unlike scrolling through social media, reading before bed can improve sleep quality because it doesn't involve blue light that disrupts our natural rhythm.

However, many people find it hard to make time for reading due to busy schedules and the attraction of digital devices. Experts suggest starting with just 15–20 minutes a day and keeping a book nearby. In conclusion, reading is a simple, affordable activity that can transform your life – so why not start today?
