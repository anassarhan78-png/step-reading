Passage 12 · My School (نصوص متكاملة + استماع)

🏫 My Wonderful School

🏛️ paragraph 1
My school is called Al-Noor International School. It is a big school with three floors. There are more than 500 students and 40 teachers. I am in grade 7, and my classroom is on the second floor.
📚 paragraph 2
We have many subjects at school. I like English and Science the most. My English teacher, Ms. Fatima, is very kind. She makes learning fun. Math is a bit difficult for me, but I try my best.
📖 paragraph 3
Our school has a big library. I go there every week to borrow books. My favorite books are adventure stories. The library also has computers. We can use them to do research for our projects.
⚽ paragraph 4
After classes, I often play with my friends in the school playground. We play football or basketball. There is also a sports club on Thursdays. I joined the football team last month. We practice every week.
🎓 paragraph 5
School is not just about studying. It is also a place to make friends and learn new things. I love my school because it feels like a second home. I am happy to go there every day.

📘 STEP‑style questions (A2) – اختبر فهمك

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does “subjects” mean?
2 Detail (paragraph 3): What type of books does the writer like best?
3 Main idea: What is the passage mainly about?
4 Detail (paragraph 4): What team did the writer join?
5 Inference: How does the writer feel about school?
