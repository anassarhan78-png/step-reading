📘 STEP Reading · A2

Passage 1 · My Last Holiday (مع الصور + استماع ناطق)

🌴 Meet Sami and his holiday to the beach

🧳 paragraph 1
Last summer, I went on a holiday with my family. We travelled to a beach city called Jeddah. We stayed there for one week. I was very excited because I love the sea.
🏨 paragraph 2
We stayed in a nice hotel near the beach. My brother and I shared a room. It had a big window with a view of the sea. Every morning, we had breakfast on the terrace.
🌊 paragraph 3
During the day, we spent most of our time at the beach. I swam in the sea and built sandcastles with my brother. The water was warm and clear. We also collected beautiful shells.
🍽️ paragraph 4
In the evenings, we went to restaurants near the corniche. We ate fresh fish and seafood. I tried a new dish called "Sayadieh". It was delicious. My father took many photos.
🌅 paragraph 5
On the last day, we watched the sunset from the beach. The sky was orange and pink. I felt sad to leave, but I was happy because we had a wonderful time. I hope we can go again next year.

📘 STEP-style questions (A2) - Interactive

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does "terrace" mean?
2 Go back to paragraph 3: What did Sami and his brother do at the beach?
3 Main idea: What is the story mainly about?
4 Detail: What did Sami try for the first time at the restaurant?
5 Inference: How did Sami feel at the end of the holiday?
