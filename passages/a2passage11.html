Passage 11 · My Pet Cat (نصوص متكاملة + استماع)

🐱 My Pet Cat, Luna

🐈 paragraph 1
I have a pet cat. Her name is Luna. She is two years old. Luna has soft white fur and big green eyes. She is small but very playful. I love her very much.
🏡 paragraph 2
We got Luna from an animal shelter last year. She was very shy at first. She hid under the sofa for two days. But after a week, she started to feel safe. Now she runs around the house all day.
🧶 paragraph 3
Luna loves to play with a small red ball. She chases it around the living room. Sometimes she brings it back to me. She also likes to climb on the sofa and watch birds outside the window.
😴 paragraph 4
At night, Luna sleeps on my bed. She curls up next to my pillow. She purrs loudly, and that helps me fall asleep. In the morning, she wakes me up by licking my hand.
❤️ paragraph 5
Having a pet is a big responsibility. I feed Luna every day and clean her litter box. But she gives me so much joy. She is not just a pet; she is part of our family. I cannot imagine life without her.

📘 STEP‑style questions (A2) – اختبر فهمك

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does “animal shelter” mean?
2 Detail (paragraph 1): What color are Luna's eyes?
3 Main idea: What is the passage mainly about?
4 Detail (paragraph 4): Where does Luna sleep at night?
5 Inference: How does the writer feel about Luna?
