Passage 11 · My Pet Cat (مع الصور + استماع ناطق)

🐱 Meet Sara and her cat Mimi

🐈 paragraph 1
My name is Sara. I have a pet cat. Her name is Mimi. She is two years old. She is white and brown.
🍲 paragraph 2
Mimi likes to eat fish and chicken. She drinks milk. I give her food every morning.
😴 paragraph 3
Mimi sleeps in a small basket. Sometimes she sleeps on my bed. She sleeps a lot during the day.
🎾 paragraph 4
I like to play with Mimi. She loves to chase a red ball. She is very funny when she runs.
❤️ paragraph 5
Mimi is my best friend. When I am sad, she comes to me. I love my cat very much.

📘 STEP-style questions (A1) - Interactive

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 1, what does "pet" mean?
2 Go back to paragraph 1: What color is Mimi?
3 Main idea: What is the story about?
4 Detail: Where does Mimi sleep?
5 Inference: Why does Sara say Mimi is her best friend?
