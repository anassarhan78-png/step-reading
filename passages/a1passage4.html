🎨 STEP Reading · A1

Passage 4 · My Favorite Hobby (مع الصور + استماع ناطق)

⭐ Let's learn about Omar's hobby

🎨 paragraph 1
My name is Omar. I am nine years old. I have a favorite hobby. I love to draw and paint. After school, I take out my colors and paper. I sit at my desk and start drawing. It makes me feel calm and happy.
🏠 paragraph 2
I like to draw many things. I draw my house, my family, and my cat. Sometimes I draw trees and flowers. I use different colors. Green for the grass, blue for the sky, and red for the apples. My mother puts my drawings on the fridge.
🏫 paragraph 3
On Saturdays, I go to an art class. My friend Ahmed goes with me. The teacher shows us new things. We learn to mix colors. We learn to draw animals. Last week, I drew a big elephant. The teacher said it was very good. I felt so proud.
👨‍👦 paragraph 4
My father loves my drawings too. He gave me a special box for my art supplies. It has pencils, erasers, and a sharpener. He also bought me a new set of colored pencils. Now I have 24 colors! I can draw better with them.
✨ paragraph 5
When I grow up, I want to be an artist. I want to paint beautiful pictures. Maybe I will have my own art show. People will come and see my work. I will paint a picture for everyone in my family. Drawing is not just a hobby. It is my dream.

📘 STEP-style questions (A1) - Interactive

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 3, what does the word "proud" mean?
2 Go back to paragraph 2: Where does Omar's mother put his drawings?
3 Main idea: What is the main idea of this story?
4 Detail: What did Omar draw last week in art class?
5 Inference: What does Omar want to be when he grows up?
