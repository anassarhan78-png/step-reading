Passage 9 · At the Restaurant (نصوص متكاملة + استماع)

🍽️ A Lovely Dinner at the Restaurant

🏨 paragraph 1
Last Friday, my family and I went to a restaurant to celebrate my mother's birthday. We chose a nice Italian place in the city center. The restaurant had soft lighting and quiet music. It felt warm and cozy.
👨‍🍳 paragraph 2
A waiter welcomed us and showed us to our table. He gave us the menus and asked what we would like to drink. I ordered a lemonade, and my parents ordered some juice. The drinks came quickly.
🍝 paragraph 3
For the main course, I ordered spaghetti with meatballs. It looked delicious and smelled wonderful. My father had a grilled chicken, and my mother chose a vegetable pizza. We all enjoyed our food very much.
🎂 paragraph 4
After the main meal, the waiter brought a surprise dessert. It was a small cake with a candle on top. The candle was for my mother's birthday. We sang "Happy Birthday" to her. She was very happy and thanked everyone.
👪 paragraph 5
The evening was perfect. We talked and laughed a lot. My mother said it was one of her best birthdays. I love spending time with my family. I hope we can go out together again soon.

📘 STEP‑style questions (A2) – اختبر فهمك

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does “menu” mean?
2 Detail (paragraph 3): What did the writer order for the main course?
3 Main idea: What is the passage mainly about?
4 Detail (paragraph 4): What was the surprise after the main meal?
5 Inference: How did the mother feel at the end of the evening?
