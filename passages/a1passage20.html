Passage 20 · My Favorite Season (مع الصور + استماع ناطق)

🌸 Meet Aisha and her favorite season

🌸 paragraph 1
Hello! My name is Aisha. I am eight years old. There are four seasons in a year. My favorite season is spring.
🌷 paragraph 2
In spring, the weather is warm and nice. The flowers start to bloom. I love seeing the red and yellow flowers in the garden.
🪁 paragraph 3
In spring, I can play outside more. I fly a kite with my brother. We also go to the park and have picnics.
🐦 paragraph 4
The birds come back in spring. I hear them singing in the morning. Sometimes I put bread for them in the garden.
😊 paragraph 5
Spring makes me happy. I like to wear light clothes. I can play outside without a jacket. Spring is a beautiful season.

📘 STEP-style questions (A1) - Interactive

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does "bloom" mean?
2 Go back to paragraph 3: What does Aisha do with her brother in spring?
3 Main idea: What is the story about?
4 Detail: What does Aisha hear in the morning in spring?
5 Inference: Why does Aisha like to wear light clothes in spring?
