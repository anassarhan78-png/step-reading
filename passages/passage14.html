Passage 14 · My Favorite Food (مع الصور + استماع ناطق)

🍕 Meet Lina and her favorite food

🍕 paragraph 1
Hello! My name is Lina. I am eight years old. I love food! My favorite food is pizza.
🍕 paragraph 2
Pizza has bread, tomato sauce, and cheese. I like pizza with mushrooms and olives. My mother puts extra cheese on top.
👩‍🍳 paragraph 3
On Friday nights, we make pizza at home. I help my mother roll the dough. We put our favorite toppings on it.
👪 paragraph 4
When the pizza is ready, we all eat together. My father and brother love it too. We laugh and talk while we eat.
😊 paragraph 5
Pizza is my favorite because it is delicious and making it with my family is fun. What is your favorite food?

📘 STEP-style questions (A1) - Interactive

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does "toppings" mean?
2 Go back to paragraph 2: What toppings does Lina like on her pizza?
3 Main idea: What is the story about?
4 Detail: When does Lina's family make pizza at home?
5 Inference: Why does Lina love pizza so much?
