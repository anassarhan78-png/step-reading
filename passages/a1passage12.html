Passage 12 · At the Supermarket (مع الصور + استماع ناطق)

🛒 Meet Noura and her mother at the supermarket

🛍️ paragraph 1
Today is Friday. Noura goes to the supermarket with her mother. They need to buy food for the week.
🧺 paragraph 2
First, they get a shopping cart. Then they go to the vegetable section. Noura's mother buys tomatoes, carrots and potatoes.
🍎 paragraph 3
Next, they go to the fruit section. Noura likes apples and bananas. Her mother puts them in a bag.
🥛 paragraph 4
In the dairy section, they get milk, yogurt and cheese. Noura also asks for chocolate milk. Her mother says yes.
💰 paragraph 5
Finally, they go to the checkout. The cashier scans all the items. Noura's mother pays with money. Then they go home with full bags.
