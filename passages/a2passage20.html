Passage 20 · My Future Dream (نصوص متكاملة + استماع)

✨ My Future Dream

💭 paragraph 1
When I close my eyes, I often think about the future. What will my life be like in twenty years? I imagine myself as a successful person. I have many dreams, and I work hard to make them come true.
👨‍⚕️ paragraph 2
My biggest dream is to become a doctor. I want to help people who are sick. I know it takes many years of study, but I am ready to work hard. My parents support me and encourage me every day.
🏡 paragraph 3
In the future, I also want to have a nice house near the beach. I love the sea and the sound of waves. I imagine living there with my family. We would spend weekends playing on the sand and swimming.
✈️ paragraph 4
I also dream of travelling to many countries. I want to see the Eiffel Tower in Paris, the pyramids in Egypt, and the Great Wall in China. Travelling will help me learn about new cultures and meet interesting people.
💖 paragraph 5
But most importantly, I want to be happy. I want to be surrounded by people I love. I hope to look back one day and feel proud of what I have done. Dreams give us hope and make life exciting. I will never stop dreaming.

📘 STEP‑style questions (A2) – اختبر فهمك

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does “support” mean?
2 Detail (paragraph 3): Where does the writer want to live in the future?
3 Main idea: What is the passage mainly about?
4 Detail (paragraph 4): Which places does the writer want to visit?
5 Inference: How does the writer feel about dreams?
