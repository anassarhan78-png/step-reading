Passage 15 · A Trip to the Zoo (نصوص متكاملة + استماع)

🐘 A Wonderful Day at the Zoo

🦒 paragraph 1
Last Saturday, my family and I went to the zoo. It was a sunny day. The zoo was very big and had many animals. I was excited because I love animals. We bought tickets and went inside.
🐻 paragraph 2
First, we saw the big animals. There were elephants, giraffes, and bears. The elephants were huge and very calm. They were eating leaves from the trees. The giraffes were so tall! I took many photos of them.
🐒 paragraph 3
Next, we went to see the monkeys. They were very funny. They jumped from tree to tree and made loud sounds. Some of them came close to the glass. My little brother laughed a lot.
🦁 paragraph 4
After that, we visited the big cats. There were lions and tigers. They were sleeping under the sun. They looked so peaceful. My father told me that lions are called the kings of the jungle.
🍃 paragraph 5
At the end, we went to the petting zoo. I fed some goats and sheep. It was fun to touch their soft fur. We also watched a bird show. The birds could talk and do tricks. It was a perfect day. I want to go to the zoo again soon.

📘 STEP‑style questions (A2) – اختبر فهمك

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what does “huge” mean?
2 Detail (paragraph 3): How did the monkeys make the writer's brother feel?
3 Main idea: What is the passage mainly about?
4 Detail (paragraph 5): What did the writer do at the petting zoo?
5 Inference: How did the writer feel about the zoo visit?
