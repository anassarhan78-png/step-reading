Passage 16 · At the Park (مع الصور + استماع ناطق)

🌳 Meet Salim and his friends at the park

🌿 paragraph 1
My name is Salim. I am seven years old. I like to go to the park near my house. I go there with my friends.
🛝 paragraph 2
At the park, there are swings and a slide. I love the swings. My friend Khalid likes the slide.
⚽ paragraph 3
Sometimes we play football on the grass. We take turns being the goalkeeper. It is very fun.
🧺 paragraph 4
After playing, we sit on a bench. We drink water and eat snacks. My mother gives me biscuits.
🌅 paragraph 5
We stay at the park until sunset. Then we go home. I always have a good time at the park.

📘 STEP-style questions (A1) - Interactive

1 Word meaning: In paragraph 2, what is a "slide"?
2 Go back to paragraph 2: What does Salim love at the park?
3 Main idea: What is the story about?
4 Detail: What do Salim and his friends do after playing?
5 Inference: How does Salim feel about going to the park?
